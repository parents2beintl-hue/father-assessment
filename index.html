How have you been doing lately?

This is a quiet space. Just you and some honest questions. No one is watching, nothing is being judged, and there are no wrong answers.

This takes about 5 minutes.

Your answers are saved privately on this device. You can stop and come back at any point.

At the end you will get a clear, honest picture of how you are doing - and what, if anything, you might want to do next. That part is always your choice.

A lot of new fathers feel something is off but cannot name it. They put their head down, keep going, and quietly fall apart. This tool exists because that should not have to happen.

Built by Sarah Bailey, Founder of Parents2Be International. NHS eLfH Perinatal Mental Health trained. This is not a clinical diagnosis. It is a starting point.

Before you start

These questions are about how things have actually been for you recently

There are 13 questions. Each one asks you to think honestly about how things have felt - not how you think they should feel, and not the version you show everyone else.

Everything you are feeling is worth taking seriously. Nothing here will be dismissed.

Some questions might feel uncomfortable. That is completely understandable. Take your time.

If you need to stop, your answers are saved. Come back whenever you are ready.
