How have you been doing lately?

This is a quiet space. Just you and some honest questions. No one is watching, nothing is being judged, and there are no wrong answers.

This takes about 5 minutes.

Your answers are private. You can skip anything you are not ready for.

At the end you will get a clear, honest picture of how you are doing - and what, if anything, you might want to do next. That part is always your choice.

A lot of new fathers feel something is off but cannot name it. They put their head down, keep going, and quietly fall apart. This tool exists because that should not have to happen.

Built by Rah, founder of Parents2Be International. NHS eLfH Perinatal Mental Health trained. This is not a clinical diagnosis. It is a starting point.

Before you start

These questions are about the past month

There are 13 questions. Each one asks you to think about how things have felt recently - not how you think they should feel, not how you are performing. Just how things actually are.

There are no right or wrong answers here.

Some questions might feel uncomfortable. That is normal. Take your time with each one.

If at any point you want to stop, you can. Come back when you are ready.
